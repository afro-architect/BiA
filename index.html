Why do Black Architects Matter?


@michaelthearkitect



Welcome to Black in Architecture [BiA]!

My name is Monica and I recently graduated with a Masters in Architecture from the University of California Berkeley.

As a first-gen, low-income student, I found architecture school very challenging and had to rely on more experienced peers for guidance, particularly given that my background wasn't in architecture, but rather in STEAM education, instructional design, and public health.

I made this site for other underrepresented students who may be considering making a shift into architecture because I couldn't find many resources from other underrepresented students in the architecture field. I'm curious to learn from others who are practicing and studying to join the field what their experiences have been like and what supports you wish you would have or need now to feel more supported in the field.

The plan is AT MINIMUM to provide lesson plans and actually helpful resources, since a lot of that information is gatekept. But ideally, if folks want to collaborate to provide this information and share their own experiences + what helped them navigate the experience, this could be a really helpful resource.

Note: I should forewarn you that I'm not a content producer, so the first few vids may look a bit raggedy while I try to figure out the best workflow and format to upload content. Gonna add feedback features at some point so we can improve your experience of the site and possibly even build a lil community.
